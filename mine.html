Arkade London

- Audio Reactive Art

Design, Art Direction / JUL 2016

Arkade London is a pure experimental real time audio-reactive experiment working with Soundcloud’s API, WebGL and the WebAudio API. It gives the power to the user and lets them interact, using their keyboard to trigger generative real time graphics and visuals on top of the music they love. It’s been designed so that it can be projected on big walls and give easy access to DJs who need graphic visuals for their shows.
